Workflow spine

A homepage rhythm that feels operational from top to bottom.

Instead of isolated feature cards, the page can move like a live fieldwork shift: launch, route, watch, close. That gives the whole site a stronger narrative spine.

Launch

Create a project, define quotas, and see the operational load before the first respondent enters.

Route

Balance suppliers and panel sources with live constraints instead of manual spreadsheet chasing.

Watch

Expose completion velocity, quality pressure, and stalled markets in a single operating view.

Settle

Close supplier batches, protect margin, and make client delivery feel controlled.
