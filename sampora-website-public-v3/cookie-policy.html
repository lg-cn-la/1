What this policy covers

This Cookie Policy explains how Sampora uses cookies, local storage, session storage, and similar browser technologies on getsampora.com.

At this stage, the website uses only necessary storage. Analytics cookies and marketing cookies are not currently enabled.

Current necessary storage

Name / typePurposeDuration
sampora_lang
localStorage		Remembers whether the visitor selected English or Chinese.Until cleared or changed by the visitor.
sampora_cookie_consent
localStorage		Remembers that the visitor has viewed or saved Cookie Preferences.Until cleared or updated.
sampora_contact_pending
sessionStorage		Used only when the contact form endpoint is not connected, to keep a fallback pending status in the current browser session.Until the browser tab/session is closed.
sampora_docs_doc
localStorage		Remembers the last selected resource manual document for basic navigation convenience.Until cleared or changed by the visitor.

Analytics cookies

Currently not used. If we enable analytics cookies in the future, they will only be loaded after we ask for consent through an updated banner or preference control.

Marketing cookies

Currently not used. If we enable advertising pixels, retargeting cookies, or campaign measurement cookies in the future, they will only be loaded after we ask for consent through an updated banner or preference control.

Cookie Preferences

You can open Cookie Preferences from the footer at any time. In the current phase, Necessary storage is always on because it supports basic site functions. Analytics and Marketing are shown as “currently not used.”

Browser controls

You can also block or delete cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Blocking necessary storage may affect language preference, cookie preference, resource manual state, or contact form fallback behavior.

Updates and contact

We may update this Cookie Policy if we add analytics, marketing, or other non-essential technologies. For questions, contact sales@getsampora.com.

Effective date: May 24, 2026

本政策说明什么

本 Cookie 政策说明 Sampora 如何在 getsampora.com 使用 Cookie、localStorage、sessionStorage 和类似浏览器技术。

当前阶段，本网站仅使用必要存储。分析 Cookie 和营销 Cookie 当前未启用。

当前必要存储

名称 / 类型用途保留时间
sampora_lang
localStorage		记住访问者选择英文或中文。直到访问者清除或更改。
sampora_cookie_consent
localStorage		记住访问者已查看或保存 Cookie 偏好设置。直到清除或更新。
sampora_contact_pending
sessionStorage		仅在联系表单 endpoint 未连接时使用，在当前浏览器会话中保存 fallback pending 状态。浏览器标签页/会话关闭后结束。
sampora_docs_doc
localStorage		记住资源手册中最近选择的文档，便于基础导航。直到访问者清除或更改。

分析 Cookie

当前未启用。未来如启用分析 Cookie，我们会通过更新后的提示或偏好控制先征得同意，再加载相关技术。

营销 Cookie

当前未启用。未来如启用广告像素、再营销 Cookie 或广告转化衡量 Cookie，我们会通过更新后的提示或偏好控制先征得同意，再加载相关技术。

Cookie 偏好设置

您可以随时通过页脚打开 Cookie 偏好设置。当前阶段，必要存储始终开启，因为它支持网站基础功能。分析和营销项目显示为“当前未启用”。

浏览器控制

您也可以通过浏览器设置阻止或删除 Cookie 与本地存储。阻止必要存储可能影响语言偏好、Cookie 偏好、资源手册状态或联系表单 fallback 行为。

更新与联系

如果我们增加分析、营销或其他非必要技术，可能会更新本 Cookie 政策。如有问题，请联系 sales@getsampora.com。
